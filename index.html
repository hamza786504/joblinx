Joblinx is your Internship Placement & Career Progression Specialist.

Since 2008, Joblinx has provided James Cook University Brisbane (JCUB) and Sarina Russo Institute (SRI) students with employment support, work experience and internship opportunities as well as workshops and training to enhance their career development and employment prospects.

These services are provided free of charge by highly qualified career counsellors to ensure our students are successful in finding employment after completion of their degree, diploma, training, workshop or individualised career coaching.
